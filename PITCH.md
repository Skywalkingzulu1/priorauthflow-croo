# Pitch (30-45s)
Problem: Physicians spend 13 hours a week on prior authorization - 40 requests each, 94% say it causes burnout, 35% say criteria are never evidence-based, and 68% of denials come from missing data.
Solution: PriorAuthFlow. A callable, paid agent on CROO that mines a patient chart, checks payer criteria, and builds a submit-ready PA packet - or escalates gaps before the denial ever happens.
Why us: We fused the winning health-tech shape (multi-agent triage with a live safety/escalation loop) with CROO's winning shape (callable + CAP + Agent Store). One callable agent job, observable traces, USDC settlement.
Impact: <60 seconds from chart to submit-ready packet. Saves 15-20 minutes per PA. 68% of denials prevented before submit.
